WHO WE ARE..!

BPPF is a dedicated organization committed to restoring balance in the clean air, water, food for present and future generation and ecosystem through activities such as forestation. Their action team is actively working in rural areas, encouraging communities to participate and achieve desired environmental outcomes. As responsible individuals, it's our duty to support this commendable initiative. Currently, a pilot project is being planned in Telangana state, marking the beginning of this significant endeavor. Your contribution can make a substantial difference in this mission, helping to create a sustainable future for all.

Our Projects

Forestation

We're dedicated to extensive reforestation to counter deforestation. Our diverse vegetation planting replenishes areas, mitigating climate change. Our grassroots initiative aims to plant 50,000 trees per village, enhancing biodiversity and air quality. We support farmers with oxygen-producing, fruit-bearing plants for sustainability, aiming to plant 100 crore trees globally.

Water Harvesting

Preserving potable water is crucial. Our initiatives focus on rainwater harvesting, improving groundwater, and preventing river water wastage. By promoting sustainable practices and community involvement, we strive to ensure clean water access for all, fostering a future where communities thrive in harmony with nature.

Education

Commercialization can hinder education's holistic development. Progressive activities fostering critical thinking are vital. BPPF aids government schools with resources for inclusive, quality education. The integration of technology, experiential learning, and environmental consciousness are essential for societal progress and empowerment.

Health

In today's hectic world, health often takes a backseat to career and social pressures. Reconnecting with cultural practices like yoga and Ayurveda, alongside promoting sustainable living, is crucial. Organizations like BPPF advocate for holistic wellness through education and community support, ensuring a healthier future for all.

Agriculture

Transitioning farmers to sustainable practices, like organic farming and GM crops, ensures food security and environmental preservation. Organizations play a key role by providing support and resources for sustainable farming methods, aiding in the resilience of food systems and ecosystem health.
